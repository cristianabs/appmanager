STORY 1

PHYSICAL INVENTORY

Hardware Inventory, a cornerstone of any Operations Support System (OSS), plays a critical role in network management. AppManager® not only delivers essential Network Element (NE), Board, and Port information but also elevates your network's visibility with advanced insights into usage, status, and its integration with the SNC Inventory.

With AppManager®, you gain more than just basic hardware data—you get a powerful tool that deepens your understanding of network performance and relationships. Ensure your operations are always a step ahead with AppManager®'s complete hardware inventory capabilities.

STORY 2

SNC INVENTORY

Logical Inventory is another foundational element of any Operations Support System (OSS). Within this framework, the SNC (Subnetwork Connection) Inventory provides crucial insights into the various connections provisioned across four layers of an Optical Network.

With AppManager®, you gain detailed visibility into SNC Inventory across DSR, OTU, OCH, and OMS layers, as well as the intricate bindings within OTN digital layers. This ensures that your network operates with precision and efficiency, delivering the clarity needed to manage complex optical networks effortlessly.

STORY 3

OPT-NET TOPOLOGY

AppManager® constructs your optical network topology by seamlessly integrating physical inventory elements—such as Network Elements (NE), cards, and ports—with the SNC inventory that defines connections. This holistic approach provides a comprehensive view of your network’s architecture.

With AppManager®'s enhanced 3D optical network topology view, you can effortlessly visualize the supported and supported links across layers, utilizing OMS SNC connections as the foundation. Additionally, AppManager® elevates route analysis by proactively segregating DSR connections into joint or disjoint paths, ensuring optimal network performance and reliability.

STORY4

OPT-NET SPECTRUM

AppManager® brings clarity to your network by binding OMS layer topology with OCH connections. This integration offers a comprehensive view of supporting OMS links, supported OCH connections, and the optical spectrum resources —whether available or currently in service— used by OCH connections.

Beyond just visualization, AppManager® enhances your network management with powerful graphical analysis of the optical spectrum between endpoints (A, B). With this detailed insight, you can make informed decisions to optimize network performance and resource allocation.

STORY5

PLANNING REPORTS

AppManager® Planning Reports serve as essential tools for evaluating both installed and provisioned capacity between two endpoints (A, B). These reports provide the foundational insights needed to ensure your network operates at peak efficiency.

AppManager® further enhances network management by introducing the FIX concept, allowing you to "label" two endpoints and use FIX to group the network resources that support connectivity across various OTN layers. This innovative approach streamlines resource management and simplifies complex network operations.

STORY6

Harness the power of real-time data like never before with AppManager®. Built on Kafka's robust technology, AppManager® seamlessly creates, manages, and transforms thousands of messages across your organization. Whether you operate with modern infrastructures or legacy networks that depend on SNMP for performance reporting, AppManager® adapts to your needs. Its powerful querying capabilities and on-demand data assembly ensure you always have the precise information you need, exactly when you need it.

STORY7

OUTSIDE PLANT

AppManager® equips you with comprehensive modeling, visualization, and reporting tools that cover every aspect of your physical layer network infrastructure. These powerful features enable strategic planning and efficient issue resolution, giving you full control over your network's operations.
