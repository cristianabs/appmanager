ENHANCING CONNECTIVITY IN PERU

REGIONAL NETWORKS IN PERU

PRONATEL (Programa Nacional de Telecomunicaciones) is committed to bridging Peru's digital divide by fostering regional network development. Despite significant urban growth, many rural and underserved areas still struggle with limited or no internet access.

This lack of connectivity severely hampers access to essential services such as healthcare, education, and government resources. PRONATEL’s initiative aims to expand high-speed internet access to these regions, promoting digital inclusion and enabling economic and social progress. This effort is part of a broader national strategy to ensure that all Peruvians, regardless of their location, can benefit from modern telecommunications infrastructure.

PRONATEL’S REQUIREMENTS

When PRONATEL awarded Telefónica del Perú (Movistar) the contract to deploy the Lambayeque Regional Network, it established several stringent requirements to ensure the project’s success and long-term viability. These requirements included:

 • Service Quality: Telefonica Movistar Peru was required to meet specific standards for internet speed, latency, and uptime, maintaining high-speed connections and minimizing downtime to ensure reliable service for users in the Lambayeque region.

 • Monitoring and Reporting: Telefonica Movistar Peru had to implement robust monitoring systems to continuously track network performance and promptly address any issues. Regular performance reports were mandated, detailing metrics such as network uptime, speed tests, and user satisfaction levels.

THRADEX’S ROLE

To meet PRONATEL’s stringent requirements, Telefonica Movistar Peru partnered with Thradex to deploy AppManager. This tool AppManager plays a critical role in fulfilling the service quality, monitoring, and reporting obligations set by PRONATEL.

• Inventory Management:
Thradex’s AppManager provided robust inventory management capabilities. It tracked the status and location of network equipment, managed stock levels, and ensured the availability of necessary components for maintenance and upgrades. This efficiency allowed Telefonica Movistar Peru to effectively manage resources and maintain the infrastructure needed to support the network and constitutes the cornerstone for Monitoring and Reporting capabilities.


• Service Quality:
AppManager enhanced service quality by providing real-time network performance monitoring. It tracked key metrics such as bandwidth usage, latency, and packet loss. By quickly identifying and addressing issues, Telefonica Movistar Peru could uphold the high standards required by PRONATEL, ensuring a reliable and high-speed internet experience for users.


• Monitoring and Reporting:
The comprehensive monitoring features of AppManager enabled continuous assessment of the network’s health. The software generated detailed performance reports that were submitted to PRONATEL, covering metrics like network uptime, traffic patterns, internet access usage trends and wireless access network speed test results.

LEVERAGING KAFKA

To meet the extensive monitoring and reporting requirements, AppManager leveraged Apache Kafka. Kafka's scalability, fault tolerance, and low-latency capabilities made it an ideal solution for processing large volumes of real-time data. AppManager efficiently handled high-throughput data streams by leveraging Kafka clusters, enabling real-time network performance analysis. This implementation ensured that any potential service issues could be promptly identified and resolved, thereby maintaining the high service quality and reliability standards PRONATEL mandated.

With the support of Thradex’s AppManager, Telefonica Movistar Peru completed a successful deployment of Lambayeque Regional Network and met effectively PRONATEL’s rigorous requirements for service quality, monitoring, and reporting. The integration of Kafka clusters significantly enhanced AppManager’s capabilities, allowing Telefonica Movistar Peru to deliver reliable, high-speed internet access to the residents of Lambayeque.
