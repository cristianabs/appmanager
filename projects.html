ENHANCING CONNECTIVITY IN PERU: THRADEX’S APPMANAGER AND TELEFONICA MOVISTAR PERU ROLE

PRONATEL's initiative to bridge Peru's digital divide seeks to extend high-speed internet access to underserved regions, promoting digital inclusion. In the Lambayeque Regional Network project, Telefonica Movistar Peru partnered with Thradex to deploy AppManager, a vital tool for meeting PRONATEL's demanding service quality, monitoring, and reporting standards.

AppManager's features, such as real-time network performance monitoring and robust inventory management, enabled Telefonica Movistar Peru to deliver reliable internet service. The integration of Apache Kafka further enhanced the tool's capabilities, ensuring efficient real-time data processing and swift issue resolution.

THRADEX FOLLOWS THE INDUSTRY LEADERS INTEGRATING AppManager® FOR ADVANCED NETWORK MANAGEMENT

Thradex has integrated AppManager® into a transport network laboratory, enhancing network visibility and operational efficiency. By leveraging standard interfaces like TAPI, NETCONF/OpenConfig, and gNMI/OpenConfig, AppManager® provides advanced features such as network inventory, optical topology visualization, and multi-vendor interoperability.

This integration aligns with industry trends towards advanced network management solutions, enabling streamlined operations and laying the foundation for future innovations.
